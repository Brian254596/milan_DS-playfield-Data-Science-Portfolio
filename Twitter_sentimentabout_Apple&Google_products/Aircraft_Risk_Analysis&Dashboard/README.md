## 1.1 Project Overview
 
 The primary objective of this project is to determine which aircrafts are the lowest risk for the company to start its venture into  purchasing and operating airplanes for commercial and private enterprises by analyzing potential risk of aircrafts

 The project will leverage data from "AviationData.csv" which contains historical aircraft data to identify lowest risk aircraft models using statistical analysis and data visualization

The data contains Injury Severity, Aircraft Dmage and Accident frequency









## 1.2 Problem Statement

The company is looking to expand it into purchasing and operating aircrafts for commercial and private enterprises but its facing uncertainity in selecting the safest and reliable aircraft without understanding the potential risk associated with different aircraft models including accidents rate, injury severity and Aircraft damage

 Without perfect understanding the company risks investing in high risk aircraft that could lead safety concerns and financial loss. This project aims to leverage aviation data to obtain meaningfull insight to identify the lowest risk aircraft models providing the company with actonable insight to make informed decisions about aircraft.








## 1.3 General Objectives








* To analyze the data and identify the riskiest and safest aircraft by make and model.

* To determine which aircraft are most and least at risk based on the purpose of flight.

* To identify the engine types associated with the highest and lowest number of accidents.

## 1.4 Expected Outcome







* A ranked list of aircraft models based on risk level, highlighting the safest and riskiest options.

* Analysis of accident risks by purpose of flight (e.g., personal, commercial, instructional).

* Identification of engine types with the highest and lowest number of accidents.

* Visualizations to support key findings and enhance decision-making.

* Actionable recommendations for selecting low-risk aircraft for commercial and private use.

## 1.5 Benefits









* Supports informed decision-making for aircraft purchases based on data-driven risk analysis.

* Reduces the likelihood of investing in high-risk aircraft, improving operational safety.

* Enhances the company’s reputation by prioritizing safety in aviation operations.

* Minimizes potential financial losses related to accidents or maintenance issues.

* Provides a strong foundation for future aviation safety assessments and fleet expansion.
